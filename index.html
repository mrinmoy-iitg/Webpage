Assistant Professor | Computer Science and Engineering | Indian Institute of Technology Jammu

I am passionate about applying machine learning and signal processing to speech-related tasks. My research focuses on areas like:
  • Speech Signal Processing: Analyzing and understanding the characteristics of speech signals.
  • Machine/Deep Learning: Utilizing AI techniques for speech applications.
  • Automatic Speech Recognition: Automatic transcription of speech.
  • Audio Classification/Detection: Identifying and categorizing audio content.
  • Speaker Verification & Identification: Distinguishing, verifying and recognizing speakers.
  • Dialect/Language Identification: Identifying the language or dialect used in speech.
  • Vocal Mode Classification: Analyzing emotional and paralinguistic information in speech.
